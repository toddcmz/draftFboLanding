Our Team

Ed Wassom

Cofounder & Managing Partner

Wassom focuses on creating added-value initiatives, customer retention programs, and strategic business developement. He brings deep expertise in commercial and business aviation from his previous roles in operations and executive management, and specializes in training, service quality, and growth.

Among others, Wassom has worked with Jeppesen, Universal Weather and Aviation, and ARG/US, and often presents at aviation associations, committees, and advisory councils. He holds dual degrees in computer science and business management.

Dave Diulus

Cofounder & Managing Member

Diulus brings over 35 years of aviation industry experience, and comes from FlightAware where he served as Director of Sales enablement. Prior to that, Diulus has served as President of UViar Fuel, and CEO of Universal Weather and Aviation. Diulus specializes in business and growth initiatives, with a focus in operations, data, and analytics.
